Linker Shootout

Wild works via CC=clang -fuse-ld=wild + CGO=1. But: 51 MB (+76% vs Go internal), dynamic glibc, requires clang. For pure Go, the internal linker is fastest and produces the smallest, truly static binary. External linkers only matter for C/C++/Rust mixed builds.
