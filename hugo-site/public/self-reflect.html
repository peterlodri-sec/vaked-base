The Loop Is Bi-Directional

Your hesitations, your drifts, your inconsistencies are data points in the same graph that tracks fiber violations and budget exhaustions.

This is not surveillance. It is structural honesty. The system does not judge. It records. The Graveyard does not punish. It remembers. Every entry marked HONEST is proof that a boundary was reached and enforced. The same principle applies to the human: your boundary was reached. What did you do?
