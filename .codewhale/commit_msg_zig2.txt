fix(openrouterd): use Zig 0.16 module-level link_libc (not exe.linkLibC)

CI caught that exe.linkLibC() does not exist on Build.Step.Compile in
Zig 0.16 (error: no field or member function named 'linkLibC'). In 0.16
libc linkage moved to the module: .link_libc = true in createModule
(std/Build/Module.zig:234). Applied to both the exe and test modules.
